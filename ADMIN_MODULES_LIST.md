# Admin Dashboard Modules - 12 Core Modules

Based on the backend extensions, the admin dashboard should have these 12 modules:

1. **Branches** - Branch/store management
2. **Inventory** - Stock management and tracking
3. **Products** - Product and variant management
4. **Orders** - Order management and fulfillment
5. **Customers** - Customer management and profiles
6. **Pricing** - Pricing rules and gold rates
7. **Taxes** - Tax configuration by region
8. **Fulfillment** - Shipping and dispatch
9. **Returns** - Returns and refunds management
10. **Promotions** - Promotions, coupons, campaigns
11. **Reports** - Analytics and reporting
12. **Settings** - System settings and configurations

Additional modules (supporting):
- **CMS** - Content management
- **Notifications** - Notification management
- **Payments** - Payment transactions
- **Invoices** - Invoice management
- **Integrations** - External integrations
- **Audit** - Audit logs
- **Permissions** - User roles and permissions
- **Regions** - Region management
- **Currency** - Currency and exchange rates
